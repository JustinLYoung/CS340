Elite Fitness is a luxury fitness center that caters to over 3,000 members of all fitness levels. A database website will help manage significant information pertaining to the Members, Trainers, Classes, and Memberships offered at this fitness center. Members are able to purchase a Gold, Platinum, or Diamond monthly membership. Elite Fitness generates an annual revenue of over $2.8MM from its membership programs. Each Memberships category offers Members to participate up to an unlimited number of Classes and receive individualized training sessions with elite Trainers that is tailored to their individual needs. The database website will also organize accurate information, such as the class (Classes) that each trainer (Trainers) teaches and the member or Members that Trainers instruct during individual sessions. Through the introduction of a database website, Elite Fitness members will have an enhanced and personalized fitness experience.
